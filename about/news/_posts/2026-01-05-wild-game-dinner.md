---

The Housatonic and Connecticut Rivers Councils are proudly hosting a Wild Game Dinner on February 28, 2026 at Camp Workcoeman where you should 'Be Prepared' to eat incredible food to support this rare opportunity for a well-done cause. Your ticket and sponsorships will support local Scouting across Connecticut! 

For more information and to purchase tickets, please visit the [registration page](https://e.givesmart.com/events/MKM/).
