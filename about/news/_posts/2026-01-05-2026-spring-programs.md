title: 2026 Winter/Spring Programs at Camp Workcoeman
---

A variety of day and weekend Scouting programs are planned for this spring at Camp Workcoeman. These activities include Scouts BSA, Cub Scout, training, and volunteer events.

Of special note is our Scouts BSA [America's Semiquincentennial Camporee]({{ site.url }}/year-round-programs/events/2026/05/15/americas-semiquincentennial-camporee/) in May.

More information and registration links are available [here]({{ site.url }}/scouts-bsa/year-round-programs/) for Scouts BSA opportunities, [here]({{ site.url }}/cub-scouts/year-round-programs/) for Cub Scout opportunities, and [here]({{ site.url }}/training/) for training opportunities. Please see the [work days]({{ site.url }}/get-involved/work-days/) page for facility maintenance volunteer opportunities, or reach out to Camp Director [Jeff Seiser](mailto:jseiser@campworkcoeman.org) to volunteer at Scouting programs.
