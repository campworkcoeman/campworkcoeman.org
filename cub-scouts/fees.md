**Cub Scout Resident Camp:**

* $525 per Scout (until June 1)
* $555 per Scout (after June 1)
* $140 per adult leader/Den Chief

**Cub Scout Mini Week:**

* $295 per Scout
* $70 per adult leader/Den Chief

**Cub Scout Day Camp:**

* $325 per Scout (until June 1)
* $335 per Scout (after June 1)
* $250 per Scout for second week
* *$25 per sibling discount*
* *$175 per Den Leader discount*

**Cub Scout Family Camp Weekend**

* Cost per Youth: $75
* Cost per Adult: $35

**Campsite Reservations:**

* $50/night

**Other Facility Reservations:**

* Rates available upon <a href="mailto:jseiser@campworkcoeman.org?subject=Summer%20Facility%20Reservations">request</a>.

### More information

For more information or questions please contact Jeff Seiser at [jseiser@campworkcoeman.org](mailto:jseiser@campworkcoeman.org).
